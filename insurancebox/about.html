In March 2016, Hangzhou Baohe Technology Co., Ltd. was formally established. The company settled in the Zhejiang Internet Finance Center and established its main Internet insurance service brand "insurance box."

In August 2016, a sub-brand “cloud protection box” was created in Beijing. "Cloud Box" focuses on IT insurance and provides professional insurance services for cloud vendors and users.

Under the wave of “Internet+”, Baohe Technology proposed a new Internet insurance service model, focusing on providing customized insurance services for Internet companies. Boxer Technology combines traditional insurance products and services with Internet scenarios and fragmented thinking, and integrates market resources to create a series of more detailed and targeted insurance products and services for Internet companies and their customers. In order to strengthen the market depth of insurance, the insurance industry to promote the development of the Internet.
